Allison McDonald

First year PhD student working with Professor J. Alex Halderman at the University of Michigan.

My research interests lie in the intersection of technology and society, particularly in the areas of privacy and security. I am interested in exploring and reducing the disconnect between how technology is designed and produced and how it is perceived, used, and legislated, both domestically and internationally.

Former Roger M Jones Fellow at the European University Viadrina, where my experiences were chronicled on the fellowship blog.
