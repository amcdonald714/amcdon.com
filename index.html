I am an Assistant Professor in the Faculty of Computing & Data Sciences at Boston University. Previously, I completed my PhD in Computer Science at the University of Michigan, advised by J. Alex Halderman and Florian Schaub.

My research interests lie at the intersection of privacy, security, and society. I am particularly interested in how technology exacerbates marginalization and impacts digital safety.

I am also a Distinguished Fellow at the Center on Privacy and Technology at Georgetown Law.
